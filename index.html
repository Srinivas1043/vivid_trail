About

What the art of photography means to me ?

Check lights... Camera... Magic! Growing up in Chennai I grew up among a lot of photographers doing different types of fancy things, from mesmerising nature photography to beautiful Wedding shoots, to awesome sports action to swanky corporate events. But for me, photography has always been my way of escaping reality. Photography to me is about capturing that one perfect moment in time and making that moment last forever. To me photography is not about lenses and apertures and focal points and lighting, to me photography means finding a moment hidden in the sands of time and make it a moment worth remembering. Time will pass, people will leave, we will grow old and hopefully when we're old we will be proud of our life looking back. I want to create that one moment for you, which you can look back upon and be proud of your life.

What does it mean to be a photographer?

A picture is worth a thousand words. A picture tells a story. The story of a moment in time. A story that years later still invokes emotions in your heart. Pictures are precious and we are fortunate enough to live in an era when we can capture memories through the artificial eyes of the camera. These eyes function pretty well but they can't see the emotions behind it, they can't capture the sudden but welcome warmth of a fairly new friend, the sensation of pride and dignity from an achievement, the feeling of bittersweet goodbyes in lonely airports at late nights. That's where I come in, I'll be there, to look for friends around you that will become family, to find those moments that'll become memories, those slices of time that'll become stories.
